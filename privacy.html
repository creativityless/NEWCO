To improve website usability, ‘T Sum uses cookies. Cookies allow a web page to recognize users' computers, to track searches of multiple pages on a web page, and to identify users returning to a website. There are cookies for technical, analytical and profiling purposes. ‘T Sum informs users that they can disable or remove cookies in their web browser by following the instructions in the links below:

  • Internet Explorer (https://support.microsoft.com/en-gb/help/17442/windows-internet-explorer-delete-manage-cookies).
  • Mozilla Firefox (https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/delete-cookies-remove-info-websites-stored).
  • Safari (https://support.apple.com/kb/PH19214?viewlocale=en_US&locale=en_US).
